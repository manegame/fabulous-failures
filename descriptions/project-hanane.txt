<h1>My Father and My Brother</h1>

<p>Hanane El Ouardani</p>

<p>
‘In My Father and My Brother’ we follow an intimate journey between a father and son. I took the role as a daughter and photographer and examined the tension between them in their differences in ideology, surrounding and expectations.<br>
<br>
A struggle between father and son in Dutch-Moroccan families can often be found. The big lack of understanding in this generation makes this complication in different perceptions. The fight of finding ‘the path’ can have a big impact in the relationship between the first and second generation. What will be passed to the third?
</p>
